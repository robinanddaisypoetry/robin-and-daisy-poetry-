Robin and Daisy Poetry Light, seasons, and the quiet courage of the soul About Poems Contact About Robin and Daisy Poetry is a home for poems written through seasons of darkness and light. These works trace a path from winter snaps of the soul to thaw, play, forgiveness, and return. They are written for those who walk quietly, feel deeply, and still choose love. Snowflakes I love all you snowflakes Each one of you so unique All shining light in your own way Some hang in the streetlight Never to touch the earth Some land in the river To become the ocean Some land on a mountain top Lasting millions of years Holding up the sky My favourite one Is the one I saw melt in my hand Forever in my memory
